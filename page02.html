No plastic utensils in the canteen

In order to reduce the plastics used in the school, especially single-use plastics, we want to ban plastic utensils in the canteen. This is the first step towards reducing single-use plastics because the canteen goes through hundreds of plastic utensils in a day, which would be unhygienic to reuse. Thus, they are forced to throw it out. Through making the Bosconians bring their own utensils, then we can help make the students practice frugality more.



Therefore,

The Bosconians will be obliged to bring alternatives to plastic utensils (i.e. bamboo straws, metal utensils, water tumblers, tupperware containers)

Guidelines

1. Plastic utensils will be banned from the canteen. No stall must offer this option.
2. The offer of metal utensils will be given by the canteen. These will be sanitized both before and after the recess, lunch, and dismissal times.
3. Bosconians will be encouraged to bring their own reusable utensils. They are not allowed to use plastic utensils. If caught, then three warnings will be given by the SAO or any administration or teachers present.
