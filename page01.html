The Proposal

A CLEAN ENVIRONMENT FOR HUMAN PROGRESS

Background & Rationale

Improper handling of solid waste is one of the most serious environmental problems, given its strong negative impact on natural resources and human health. Therefore, it is necessary to promote a culture in schools, aimed at alleviating the environmental and social problems caused by garbage especially plastics.

In DBTC, the canteen continues to sell products using non-biodegradable containers. Also, they supply disposable plastic utensils which is unnecessary. Students should be strictly advised to bring their own metal utensils to lessen the plastic usage in DBTC. As a student, a part of what we need to learn in school is discipline which can be related to prudence and frugality. With proper and strict implementation and guidance, students will have the knowledge and initiative to look after the environment and be thrifty.




Main Objectives

  • To achieve waste reduction

  • To elevate environmental awareness at schools

  • To improve the conditions, quality of life and health of students and residents in general, as school waste is a source of disease and latent pollution.

  • Reduce usage of plastics within the school

  • Make DBTC an environment friendly school

  • Improve students’ awareness on sustainable development

  • Instill a mindset of prudence and frugality in students







We aim for a plastic free environment inside DBTC

We also aim to encourage environmental frugality within the school, which will instill both the value of discipline and a heightened awareness of the environment

Therefore, these are the benefits that will be provided by our projects




  • Ensures safety and health of the institution

  • Reduce the school’s environmental pollution

  • To conserve energy and help the environment

  • Reduce usage of plastics within the school

  • Make DBTC an environment friendly school

  • Keeps the school neat and clean

  • Makes it less prone to floods
